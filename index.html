The Problem We're Solving

Fashion emits 2.1B tonnes CO₂ annually. Most brands don't know their true footprint.

No standardized data

No standardized product-level carbon data exists across fashion supply chains.

Manual LCAs are inefficient

Traditional lifecycle assessments are expensive, slow, and don't scale.

Regulatory pressure

EU PEF, CSRD, and SBTi regulations demand automated traceability.

Proof, not promises

Customers & investors demand verifiable sustainability data.

What PathCarbon Does

AI-powered, product-level carbon footprinting—from fibre to fashion

Brands input material, SKU, and sourcing info → we map emissions across the supply chain

1

Input Product Data

Enter SKU details, fabric composition, sourcing regions, and product specifications.

2

Supply Chain Modeling

We map emissions across Fibre → Yarn → Fabric → Garment production stages.

3

Auto-Calculated Footprint

Emissions calculated using verified databases (ecoinvent, GHG Protocol).

4

Dashboard & Reports

Interactive analytics and downloadable reports for compliance and storytelling.
