PathCarbon

Carbon Intelligence for the Apparel and Footwear Industry

What We Do

PathCarbon is an AI-powered carbon accounting platform designed for the apparel and footwear industry. We provide science-based emissions data, PEF-aligned LCA models, and actionable intelligence for brands aiming to meet climate targets.

Who It's For

Fashion brands, ESG teams, sustainability officers, product designers, and supply chain leaders looking to decarbonize their value chains.

How It Works

We combine curated supply chain activity data, ecoinvent inventory, and advanced AI models to deliver precise carbon footprint estimates—stage by stage. Our tool aligns with Product Environmental Footprint (PEF) and GHG Protocol standards.

About

Founded by Kamil, an LCA and productivity analysis expert with deep experience in carbon accounting for fashion and food systems, PathCarbon blends domain knowledge with modern AI to drive actionable impact.
